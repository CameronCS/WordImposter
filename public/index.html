How to Play

1
Get Your Word

All players except one get the same word. The imposter gets the opposite word!

2
Describe It (60s timer)

Take turns describing your word in 50 characters or less. Don't say the word itself!

3
Vote for the Imposter

After everyone describes, vote who you think is the imposter. Skip votes and ties = no elimination!

4
Win Conditions

Play continues for multiple rounds until someone wins!
